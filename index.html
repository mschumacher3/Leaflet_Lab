Give Them More Green:

Advocating For More Funding to the National Parks Service

Over the last few decades, the United States National Park's "To-Do List" as incresed. This list includes many repairs on the thousands of miles of roads, trails, and shelters that the National Parks Service maintaines, but has not been paralleled by an increase in budget. With in increase in the number of visitors at the park each year, infrastructure is deteriorating and must be kept up to support the increasing number of visitors .

In 2015, 307,247,252 people visited the National Parks in the United States.
